Net Neutrality Info
Why this app exists.
This app was made to inform people about the immense power ISPs have without the existence of Net Neutrality.
In December 2017, the FCC voted to remove laws put in place by the Obama administration to ensure the internet is a free and open place, without restriction from ISPs.
Within the next few years, a browser like this could become the norm.
Get more info at the EFF's Battleforthenet.com
